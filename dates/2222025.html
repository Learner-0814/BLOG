System Performance Summary

Components & Performance Overview

  • Monitor: 1080p or 1440p (at least 60Hz) – Best paired with GTX 1080 or above for smooth performance.
  • CPU: AMD Ryzen 5 2600 – Decent for gaming but may bottleneck at high FPS in CPU-heavy games.
  • Motherboard: B450M / B550 / B660 – Supports Ryzen 5 2600 and offers good expansion options.
  • RAM: 16GB DDR4 3600MHz (Dual Channel) – Good for gaming; optimal speed for Ryzen is 3200MHz.
  • GPU: Varies (GTX 960, 1050 Ti, 1080, 1080 Ti) – Determines game performance (see table below).
  • Storage: 256GB NVMe + 1TB HDD – Fast boot times and ample space for games, but RDR2 and GTA 5 need SSD space for best loading times.
